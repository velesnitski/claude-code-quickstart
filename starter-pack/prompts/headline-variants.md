# Three on-brand headline variants

**When to use:** A feature launch / blog post / landing page where you want a few options to A/B test, all in your voice.

**Copy this into Claude:**

```text
Write 3 headline variants for <topic / feature name>.

Constraints:
- Use the CLAUDE.md voice rules.
- Sentence case (not Title Case).
- Under 10 words each.
- Three different angles: (1) benefit, (2) skeptical, (3) story-led.

Show only the three headlines, one per line, no commentary.
```

**Tip:** Once you have 3 you like, ask Claude to "give me 5 more in the same style, but spicier" to keep exploring without losing voice.
